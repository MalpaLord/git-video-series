Swords 'n Magic and Stuff

Swords 'n Magic and Stuff is a casual, multiplayer, open world RPG! Collect tons of weapons, spells, mounts and gear through quests, shops and crafting, level up 6 different classes and buy land to build and decorate your own house to settle down in the world of Tirawyn. This Video Series goes over the development of the game, its ups, downs and the building blocks of the game.
